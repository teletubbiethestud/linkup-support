LinkUp is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within the app.

1. Data Collection & Storage

Local Storage: All data you enter regarding "Friends" (names and addresses) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have servers, and we cannot access, see, or sell this data.

2. Location Services

LinkUp requests access to your precise location to identify nearby points of interest and calculate travel times. This location data is processed in real-time and is not stored or shared for tracking purposes.

3. Third-Party Services (AI)

To provide intelligent recommendations, LinkUp utilizes Apple Intelligence.

4. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally, deleting the LinkUp app will permanently delete all addresses and preferences stored within it.
